The Technology

Blood Flow as Identity

Photoplethysmography (PPG) is the optical blood flow sensor already built into every smartwatch and fitness band. idamond turns that existing hardware into a continuous, passive biometric — as unique as a fingerprint, without any of the privacy risks.

How It Works

01

Wear the Device

User puts on their wearable — smartwatch, fitness band, smart ring, or earbuds. No app interaction, no PIN, no fingerprint scan.

→
02

Blood Flow Continuously Captured

The device's existing heart-rate sensor reads the user's unique blood-flow signature in real time using standard PPG (Photoplethysmography) hardware — no additional sensors needed.

→
03

Identity Verified

The PPG signal is matched against a locally stored, revocable biometric template. Bluetooth or NFC signals authentication to any paired device.

→
04

Access Granted

Seamless unlocking of phones, computers, doors, payment terminals, and digital services — zero extra steps.

See It in Action

Innovation 1

Zero-Step 2FA (0S-2FA)

idamond breaks the traditional security-vs-usability tradeoff. By authenticating continuously while the device is worn, it provides two-factor security with zero user steps — something no existing solution achieves.

  • Factor 1: PPG biometric (something you are)
  • Factor 2: Physical wearable device (something you have)
  • User action required: none
Innovation 2

Revocable Biometric Templates

Unlike fingerprints and facial data, idamond biometric templates are non-time-persistent. They naturally degrade after ~3 days of non-use, making them impossible to permanently steal.

  • Templates expire autonomously — no manual revocation needed
  • New template generated each re-enrolment
  • Breach-resilient: leaked templates cannot be replayed
Innovation 3

Universal Credential Replacement

One wearable device replaces passwords, PINs, smart cards, and physical keys across all use cases — online, offline, physical access, and IoT.

  • Contactless payments & digital signatures
  • Physical access control
  • Online & offline identity verification
  • Metaverse & IoT peripheral authentication

PPG Signal Properties

Uniqueness

Subject A's PPG pattern ≠ Subject B's pattern. Blood flow topology is individual-specific and impossible to mimic.

Stability

A user's PPG at time T matches at T+10 minutes. Continuous authentication is reliable within any active session.

Revocability

A user's PPG at time T ≠ at T+3 days. Templates expire naturally, enabling inherent session timeouts and leak protection.
